Volunteer for "Walking the Camino"

Thanks for your interest in helping make Walking the Camino a success; It wouldn’t be possible without you! Here’s how you can help:

  1. If you live near a city where the film will be screening:
    1. Work with our team to distribute flyers and postcards
    2. Create a list of neighborhoods to distribute flyers and postcards
    3. Post the flyer & trailer on your Facebook wall, email it to your personal contacts
    4. Create a MeetUp group, announce it at your church, gym, etc., contact the press
  2. If you don’t live near a city where the film will be screening but still want to help:
    1. Create an outreach list for a city we’re screening in Contact Outreach (Honi & Maggie) at outreach@caminodocumentary.org for flyers, postcards, and other ways to help get the word out.

A thousand thanks!!
