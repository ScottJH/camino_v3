Share “Walking the Camino” With Your Community!

"Walking the Camino” has joined forces with Tugg.com - a web-platform that helps individuals, groups, and organizations setup personalized movie screenings – to help share the film with audiences across the country!

Two Ways to Host A Screening through Tugg.com:

At Your Local Movie Theater:

  1. Fill out the Event Request Form and select the theater, date, and time.

  2. After theater approval, your Event Page can begin reserving tickets.

  3. Share the event with your network and confirm your screening by selling enough tickets before the deadline.

  4. Sit back and enjoy the show!

At Your Community Venue or Campus:

  1. Choose a date, time, and place to show the film.

  2. Purchase a screening license

  3. Use Tugg’s ticketing solution to manage RSVPs, sell tickets, or fundraise.

  4. Sit back and enjoy the show!

Help “Walking the Camino” reach audiences across the US by hosting a screening in your area! Visit Tugg to get started!

Need help? E-mail communityscreenings@tugginc.com and someone will be happy to walk you through the process!
